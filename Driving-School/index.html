About eye teach school of driving

With over 30 years of driving instruction, we are committed to getting you on the road and behind the wheel! Instructors are certified and licensed by the State of Ohio. We take the stress out of learning to drive as our instructors strive to build relationship, breakdown content and provide you with the tools to become a successful driver. We are also able to create an individualized program based on your needs. Our goal is to help new teenage drivers become safe, confident drivers.

Teen drivers who graduate from our school are equipped with the knowledge and experience required to be collision-free and ticket-free in their driving. We also strive to provide instruction to adults who need remediation or are first time drivers.

OHIO STATE LAW

Ohio State Law requires that drivers 15 1/2 to 18 years of age take 24 hours of In-Class instruction and at least 8 hours of behind the wheel instruction.

REMEDIAL COURSES

We also provide remedial courses for adults who have been mandated by the courts to take coursework in order to get their licenses reinstated.
